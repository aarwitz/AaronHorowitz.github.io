Truss Modeling in MATLAB

Published on February 4, 2022

This program enables its user to generate a graphical and mathematical model of a 2d truss. Functions for adding/removing/moving truss joints and beams allows the user to manipulate the truss, and shift the distribution of weight to optimize the load-cost ratio. The force in each beam is calculated and displayed alongside the visual model, providing a clean interface to build the truss. Specification-checking function ensures that the truss meets any desired standards, with respect to characteristics from its dimensions to its cost.
