My name is Aaron Horowitz. I am from Boca Raton, Florida and currently living in Boston where I work as a software engineer at Cognex.

I have a B.S. in Finance and an M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Boston University. I was a research assistant at the BU Robotics Lab, where I learned the basics of labeling large datasets and computer vision. I also like writing essays on topics I am curious about, usually related in some way to philosophy of mind.

I made this website because I wanted to record my progress in building things and writing my ideas down.
